About me

I completed the Web development training of Microverse. I have thousands of hours in pair programming and remote collaboration. I used to work in global, fast-paced, and diverse environments.

I create web applications using programming languages and frameworks such as JavaScript, Node.js, React.js, Ruby, and Ruby on Rails. I have a solid foundation in data analysis with Excel, Python, and R.

From my 12 years of career in the Haitian public administration - which peaks as the head of the Statistic and planning department in CONATEL ( the Haitian telecom regulatory agency), I gained professional skills in team and time management, and public speaking, and negotiation. I am a polyglot who speaks English and french fluently. I am an avid reader and a lifelong learner. I enjoy creative coding and I am a generative art fan. If you'd like to get in contact about potential opportunities, please reach out through my email: lafontant.david@gmail.com.
